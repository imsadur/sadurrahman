Overview

Messaging that thinks with you.

Pops is an AI-powered messaging app that goes beyond sending text. It understands context, learns communication patterns, surfaces smart replies, and helps groups coordinate more effectively. I led the full product design — from initial concept through to a shippable high-fidelity prototype covering all primary user flows.

The core design challenge: AI features in messaging feel intrusive or gimmicky when done wrong. The goal was to make intelligence feel invisible — present when useful, absent when not. Every AI touchpoint had to earn its place in the flow.

Design Principle

AI that earns its place.

Most AI messaging features are designed to impress engineers, not users. They're visible, interruptive, and often wrong. I designed Pops around a different principle: the AI should feel like a good friend who knows when to speak and when to stay quiet.

Smart replies appear only when the conversation has reached a natural reply point. Context summaries surface only when returning to a long thread. The AI works in the background, and surfaces only when the user clearly benefits.
