I design products people trust.

I'm Sadur Rahman — a Product Designer based in Bengaluru with 12 years of experience. I don't have one domain. I have a way of working: understand the user, understand the business, then design something that earns trust.

My career spans fintech, SaaS, eCommerce, travel, consumer apps, B2B platforms, messaging, and DeFi. From Paytm Money (1M+ users) to Tapzo (acquired by Amazon), HDFC Securities, and now Aspora — I've shipped at every scale.

I also do branding, logo design, illustration, and marketing collateral for clients across India and internationally.

Currently building AI-native prototypes with Claude, Cursor, and Lovable — from idea to tested prototype faster than any traditional sprint.
