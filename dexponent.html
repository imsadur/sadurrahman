Overview

Institutional DeFi, made usable.

Dexponent is a decentralised platform for institutional-grade investment funds and strategies — bringing benchmarking, verifiability, and performance monitoring to on-chain finance, with principal asset protection built in. The platform lets fund managers launch, raise, and run tokenised stock indexes and managed DeFi funds permissionlessly.

I was hired as Lead Product Designer at Treflo to own the full UX of the Dexponent testnet — staking flows, fund launch tools, trading dashboards, and the yield strategy interface. Under aggressive timelines, I used AI tools throughout to move faster than a traditional sprint would allow.

The Product

Launch, Raise & Run.

Dexponent's core value proposition is enabling anyone to launch an on-chain investment fund with institutional-grade safeguards — LP safety enforced by code, an audit trail on-chain, and benchmark-verified performance. The FarmWars competitive mode added a gamified yield competition layer that drove testnet adoption.

AI-First Design Process

Using AI as a design collaborator.

With a short runway to testnet launch, I embedded AI tools at every stage — not as a shortcut, but to expand the design space faster than any solo designer could alone. I used Figma AI for wireframe generation, ChatGPT for UX copy and flow logic, and user-tested the top 5 of 30+ prompt-engineered concepts.
